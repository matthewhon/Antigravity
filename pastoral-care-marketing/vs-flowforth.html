Common Questions

Frequently asked

Do you integrate with Planning Center?

Yes — deeply. We sync People, Groups, Services, Giving, Check-ins, and Registrations from Planning Center. We also write data back (update person records, log care notes) and support PCO webhooks for near-real-time updates. Flowforth only syncs people and email lists.

We already use Flowforth for email. Can we add your platform on top?

You can, but most churches find they switch fully once they see our email builder. We offer the same core email functionality — plus SMS, polls, forms, and analytics — all in one platform. No need to manage two tools, two logins, or two billing lines.

How does the SMS feature work?

We partner with SignalWire to provide dedicated phone numbers for your church. You can send broadcasts to PCO lists, manage inbound conversations in a shared team inbox, set up keyword auto-responses, and enable our AI agent to handle replies automatically. Team members get role-based access so pastoral staff sees only relevant conversations.

What are the live polls used for?

Churches use them during Sunday services (sermon topic votes, prayer request collection), small groups, town halls, and leadership meetings. The projector mode shows results live on your big screen — congregation members participate by scanning a QR code on their phone.

How does pricing compare to Flowforth?

Flowforth charges per email sent, so your costs scale with send volume. We charge a flat monthly subscription with no per-message fees for email, and transparent per-SMS rates. For most active churches, we’re more predictable and often less expensive once you factor in SMS and the analytics tools you’d otherwise pay separately for.

Can we migrate from Flowforth?

Yes. Our onboarding team will help you export your unsubscribe lists from Flowforth, import them into our platform, and sync your PCO data — typically in under 30 minutes. We’ll make sure no one gets emailed who has already opted out.
